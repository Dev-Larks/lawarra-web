Recovery Drink
Ingredients
    1½ tablespoons Cocoa powder
    1 tablespoon Sugar or Honey
    1½ teaspoons Chia seeds
    1½ teaspoons Flaxseed meal
    2½ teaspoons Hemp seeds
    1 3/4 cups Coconut water
    ¼ cup warm Water
    1 Banana (Optional)

Instructions
  1. Whisk ¼ cup warm Water with ingredients.
  2. Let rest for 10 minutes so Chia seeds activate.
  3. Add Coconut water and whisk thoroughly.
  4. Store in fridge
