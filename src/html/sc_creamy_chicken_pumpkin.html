Creamy Chicken with Pumpkin and Mushrooms
Ingredients
    4 Hickory smoked shortcut bacon rashers
    8 Chicken thighs
    2 tbs Flour
    1 tbs Olive Oil
    1 Brown Onion, diced
    2 Garlic cloves, minced
    300g Mushrooms, thickly sliced
    700g Butternut pumpkin, cut into 2cm cubes
    1 tbs Ground paprika
    ½ cup Chicken stock
    ⅓ cup Pouring cream
    Chopped flat leaf parsley

Instructions
  1. Heat oil, cook bacon for 5 minutes over medium heat or until golden, transfer to slow cooker.
  2. Place chicken and flour into large bowl and toss to coat, shaking off excess.
  3. Cook chicken in batches, turning for 5 minute or until browned all over.
  4. Transfer chicken to slow cooker with onion, garlic, mushroom, pumpkin, paprika and stock. Cook for 6 hours on low.
  5. Stir the cream into the slow cooker and sprinkle with parsley.
