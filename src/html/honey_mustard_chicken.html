Honey Mustard Chicken
Ingredients
    1 tsp sweet Paprika
    4 Chicken thighs
    2 Bacon rashers
    2 tbsp gluten free Flour
    160 ml Chicken stock
    2 tbsp Wholegrain Mustard
    1 tbsp Dijon Mustard
    2 tbsp Honey
    Â½ cup frozen peas
    2 tbsp cooking Cream
    2 tbsp chopped fresh flat leaf parsley

Instructions
  1. Combine oil and paprika in a large bowl.
  2. Add chicken and toss to coat.
  3. Heat large non-stick fry pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook turning for 5 minutes or until browned. Transfer to plate.
  4. Add bacon and cook stirring for 5 minutes or until golden and crisp.
  5. Place flour in slow cooker slowly whisk in stock, mustards, and honey until smooth.
  6. Add chicken and bacon and gently stir to combine.
  7. Cook on low for 4 hours.
  8. Stir in the peas and cream in the last 10 minutes of cooking.
  9. Turn off heat, replace the lid, and leave to stand for 10 minutes.
  10. Serve chicken sprinkled with parsley
