Grilled Pumpkin, Beetroot & Haloumi Salad

Ingredients

    500g Kent pumpkin cut into 6 wedges
    Â¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
    1 tbs ground Cumin
    225 grams haloumi, sliced
    1 punnet thyme, leaves picked
    60g baby rocket
    250g whole cooked beetroot, quartered
    1/3 cup pistachio kernels, toasted, chopped
    2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Preheat a barbecue grill or chargrill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush pumpkin with 1 tbs of the oil. Sprinkle with cumin. Add to grill.
  3. Cook for 8 minutes each side or until browned and cooked. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Add haloumi to grill. Cook for 1 minute each side or until golden. Transfer to a plate.
  5. Whisk remaining oil with thyme in a small bowl.
  6. Arrange rocket on a large platter. Top with beetroot, pumpkin and haloumi.
  7. Sprinkle with pistachio. Serve drizzled with oil mixture and balsamic glaze.
