Slow Cooked Beef Stew
Ingredients
    1.3kg Chuck beef, cut into 4 equal pieces
    1 tbsp Olive oil
    Freshly ground salt and pepper
    1½ cups Beef stock
    1 cup Red wine
    2 tbsp Tomato paste
    1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    ½ tbsp Balsamic vinegar
    1 tsp dried Thyme
    1 tsp salt
    6 cloves of Garlic
    1 large Onion, cut into chunks
    4 large Carrots, peeled cut into 1.2cm diagonal slices
    500g of Potatoes, diced into 1.2cm cubes
    ¼ cup of Flour
    1 cup frozen Peas

Instructions
  1. Add 1 tbsp olive oil to pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef season with salt and pepper, brown for 5 minutes in batches, and transfer to slow cooker.
  3. Add beef stock, red wine, tomato paste, worcestershire sauce, balsamic vinegar, thyme and salt and pepper.
  4. Combine with beef and than add garlic, onion chunks, carrots and diced potatoes.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours
  6. Remove 1 cup of beef broth and whisk ¼ flour until there are no lumps remaining.
  7. Add back to the slow cooker and stire to combine.
  8. Add frozen peas and cook uncovered on high for 10-15 minutes.
