Chilli Con Carne
Ingredients
    1 large Onion, diced or thinly sliced
    1 red Capsicum, diced
    2 Garlic cloves
    1 teaspoon Paprika
    1 teaspoon Cumin
    ½ teaspoon Chilli powder (mild)
    750g minced Beef
    300ml Beef stock
    400g can chopped Tomatoes
    ½ teaspoon dried Majoram
    2 tbsp Tomato puree
    400g can red Kidney beans

Instructions
  1. Add onion and cook, stirring regularly for 5 minutes or until soft.
  2. Add garlic, red pepper, chilli powder, paprika, and cumin.
  3. Give ingredients a good stir and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add mince and cook for at least 5 minutes until browned.
  5. Add beef stock, chopped tomatoes, marjoram, sugar, salt and pepper, and tomato puree.
  6. Simmer gently for 20 minutes with the lid on.
  7. Drain and rinse red kidney beans and add them to the chilli.
  8. Bring to the boil and gently simmer for 10 minutes without the lid.
  9. Turn off heat, replace the lid, and leave to stand for 10 minutes.
  10. Serve hot with your choice of toppings:
      11. Grated cheese, fresh coriander (cilantro), sliced avocado, sour cream, guacamole, etc.
