Healthier Dairy-Free Veggie Quiche
Ingredients
    5ml olive oil cooking spray
    8 free range eggs
    2/3 cup rice milk
    1 tbs Dijon mustard
    1 punnet cherry tomatoes, halved
    1 zucchini, thickly sliced
    3 slices Royal Berkshire leg ham

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan-forced. Spray a 26cm (4cm deep) round quiche dish with oil.
  2. Whisk together eggs, soy milk and mustard in a large bowl. Season with pepper.
  3. Place tomato,zucchini, and ham in dish and pour over egg mixture.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until light golden and cooked through. Season with pepper.
  5. Cool for 15 minutes and serve
