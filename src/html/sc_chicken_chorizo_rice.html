Slow Cooker Chicken & Chorizo Rice
Ingredients
    800g Chicken thigh fillets, cut into 3cm pieces
    2 Chorizo sausages, coarsely chopped
    2 tbsp Olive oil
    1 large brown Onion
    2 cloves of Garlic
    1 red Capsicum
    1 tbsp ground Paprika
    1 tbsp ground Cumin
    ½ cup (125ml) dry white wine
    1 cup rice
    1 ¾ cups Chicken stock

Instructions
  1. Add 1 tbsp olive oil to pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook chicken in batches, stirring for 2 minutes or until browned. Transfer to plate
  3. Cook chorizo, stirring for 2 minutes, transfer to plate with chicken.
  4. Cook onion and garlic for 2 minutes or until soft.
  5. Add capsicum, paprika, and cumin, cook for 1 min or until aromatic, add wine and bring to boil.
  6. Stir in rice, transfer to pre-heated slow cooker.
  7. Stir in chicken, chorizo, and stock and cook for 4 hours or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
  8. Serve with sliced black olives and chopped flat leaf parsley.
