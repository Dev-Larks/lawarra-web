Paprika Pork
Ingredients
    1 tsp Oliv Oil
    2 Onions, finely sliced
    400g pork fillet, trimmed of any fat, cut into thick strips
    250g mushrooms, sliced
    200 ml Chicken stock
    1 tbsp Tomato puree
    100ml Sour cream
    1Â½ tbsp Smoke paprika
    Tagliatelle or rice to serve

Instructions
  1. Heat oil in pan, add onions and cook for 10 mins.
  2. Add the pork and mushrooms and cook on high heat 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add paprika and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and add stock, simmer for 5-8 minutes until pork is cooked through.
  5. Mix in sour cream and serve.
