Smokey Pork and Black Bean Casserole
Ingredients
    1 tbs Olive Oil
    800g Pork shoulder steakes, trimmed and chopped
    2 Cloves carlic
    3 Medium onions, peeled and chopped
    1 tbs Smoked paprika
    2 tsp Chipotle sauce
    2 tbs Red wine vinegar
    2 tbs Tomato puree
    1 can Black beans
    1 can Mexican bean mix
    2â…“ cups Chicken stock

Instructions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 140 degrees (fan-forced).
  2. Heat oil on medium-high heat, add pork chunks and cook for 3-4 minutes until sealed on all sides.
  3. Add garlic, chipotle, tomato puree, and smoked paprika and cook for 2 minutes, stiring to coat.
  4. Stir in chicken stock and bring to simmer.
  5. Transfer to casserole dish, cover leaving a small gap and cook in oven for 2 hours.
  6. Add beans and cook uncovered for an additional 30 minutes until sauce has reduced.
