Creamy Chicken, Pumpkin & Mushrooms
Ingredients
    1 tbsp Olive Oil
    1 Brown Onion, diced
    2 Garlic cloves, minced
    4 Hickory smoked bacon rashers
    6 Chicken thighs
    300g Mushrooms
    700g Butternut pumpkin, peeled, seeded, cut into 2cm pieces
    1 tbsp ground Paprika
    ½ cup (125ml) Chicken stock
    2 tbsp of plain flour
    ⅓ cup (80ml) cream
    Chopped flat leaf parsley

Instructions
  1. Cook bacon over medium heat for 5 mins, transfer to slow cooker.
  2. Place chicken and flour in large bowl and toss to coat.
  3. Heat oil in pan and cook, turning, for 5 mins or until browned all over, transfer to slow cooker.
  4. Add onion, garlic, mushrooms, pumpkin, paprika, and stock.
  5. Cover and cook for 6 hours on low.
  6. Stir the cream into the slowcooker ingredients.
  7. Server and sprinkle with parsley.
