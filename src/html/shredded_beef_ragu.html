Shredded Beef Ragu Sauce
Ingredients
    1.2kg Chuck beef, cut into 4 equal pieces
    3 tbsp Olive oil
    3 Cloves garlic
    1 Onion
    1 cup Carrots, diced
    1 cup Celery, diced
    800g crushed canned Tomatoes
    3 tbsp Tomato paste
    2 cups Beef stock
    1 cup red Wine, Merlot
    1½ cups Water
    ¾ tsp dried Thyme
    3 dried Bay leaves
    Pappardelle, or other pasta of choice

Instructions
  1. Pat beef dry and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil over high heat, add beef and sear agressively on both sides until browned (3-5 minutes).
  3. Turn heat down to medium-low, add remaining olive oil.
  4. Add onion and garlic and sauté for 2 mins.
  5. Add the carrots and celery and sauté slowly for 5 mins.
  6. Turn heat up on high, add wine, stir and simmer for 3 mins.
  7. Then transfer all ingredients to the slow cooker.
  8. Add all remaining ingredients, but DO NOT add water.
  9. Slow cook on low for 6-8 hours, then shred beef.
  10. Cook pasta, while pasta cooks take 5 cups of the ragu and put it into a pan
  11. When the pasta is ready, transfer it directly from the pot into the fry pan using tongs.
  12. Add 3/4 cup of pasta water into the fry pan.
  13. Gently toss the pasta (I use 2 wooden spoons) for 1 to 2 minutes, until the sauce water evaporates and leaves you with a thick Ragu sauce that coats the pasta.
